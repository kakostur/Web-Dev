Welcome to the XG Fan Page

XG, short for Xtraordinary Girls, is a global girl group that debuted in 2022. Known for their versatile talents and unique style, they have captured the hearts of fans worldwide.

The group’s popular tracks include "Tippy Toes" and "Mascara," which showcase their strong vocals, synchronized choreography, and charismatic stage presence. Follow XG on their journey to make history in the K-pop industry!
